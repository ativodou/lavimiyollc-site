Who We Are

Built from roots.
Built for the diaspora.

LavimiyòLLC is a Haitian-owned company based in Pembroke Pines, Florida, created to serve the global Haitian community through authentic products, knowledge, and technology — all rooted in Haitian culture.

"To celebrate, preserve, and share Haitian culture with the world — one product at a time."
