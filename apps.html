Anbyans
Tikè Lakay · Event Ticketing

The first event ticketing platform built specifically for the Haitian community. Organizers can sell tickets, manage floor plans, and accept MonCash — Haiti's mobile payment — alongside Stripe.

  • MonCash + Stripe dual payment processing
  • QR code tickets with live door scanning
  • Interactive floor plan editor
  • Multi-role system: Organizer, Vendor, Door, Fan
  • Trilingual interface: Kreyòl, English, French

Ayisyen Tou Patou
Global Haitian Professional Directory

A living directory of Haitian professionals, organizations, and federations across the world. GPS-filtered search connects the diaspora — from Miami to Montreal to Paris — to each other.

  • GPS-based location filtering by city and country
  • Organizational and federation management
  • Member enrollment and verification
  • Community filtering by sector and specialty
  • Login history and activity tracking
